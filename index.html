01 — 关于About

以鲁迅文化为核
探索非遗的数字新生 Rooted in Lu Xun Culture
Exploring the Digital Renaissance of Intangible Heritage

本科就读于鲁迅美术学院，学业成绩位列年级前 2%，获本校研究生保送资格。核心研究方向为文创视角下的非遗数字传播。

在校期间，曾参与多项鲁迅文化相关课题研究，对这一领域有持续的关注与积累。近年来以鲁迅文化为核心进行创作，完成文创设计、纪念包装、展演视觉、动态海报等项目，并推动部分作品落地实践。

2020–2026 年间，作品在中国之星设计奖、全国美术院校大赛、新加坡金沙艺术设计大赛等赛事中有所收获，涉及红色文创、电商文创等不同方向。

Undergraduate at Luxun Academy of Fine Arts, ranking top 2% in grade. Recommended for direct postgraduate admission. Core research: digital communication of intangible cultural heritage from a cultural-creative perspective.

Participated in multiple Lu Xun culture research projects. Recent work focuses on cultural creative design, commemorative packaging, exhibition visuals, and dynamic posters—with several pieces entering commercial production.

Between 2020–2026, works received recognition at the China Star Design Award, National Art Colleges Competition, and Singapore Sands Art & Design Competition, spanning red-culture creative products and e-commerce creative directions.
